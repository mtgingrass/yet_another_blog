Setup

Whenever you purchase something that requires a setup, you always dred what is to come! This was different. As soon as I opened up the package I was in relief. I immediately tossed the instructions aside and did what every man does, assemble on my own.

A few things to take notice with assembly:

  • Actual stands came fully assembled

  • No tools, whatsoever, are required

  • Assembly is completely intuitive

The only part I had to do some “thinking” on was when I assembled the rod that goes across the top. Two of the four rods have slightly larger holes on one end. Those are the outer rods that connect to the stands. I happened to have one of them as an inner rod. Quick fix and it was fully assembled.

  • Total time to assemble, without the video review, i’m guessing 5 to 7 minutes max

  • Reassembly time if you had to do it again, 3 minutes max.
